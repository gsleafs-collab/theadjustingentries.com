About

Hi, I'm Gurjot Mann, CPA.

I'm a Chartered Professional Accountant working in the field, with deep roots in Canadian corporate, trust, and personal tax. Day to day I work on financial reporting, review, and audit engagements, preparing and reviewing working papers, performing substantive testing, and helping put financial statements together.

Before assurance, I spent years in corporate, trust, and personal tax compliance, compilations, and messy reconciliations, working heavily in CaseWare, CaseView, and various tax and accounting softwares. The Adjusting Entries is where I write the practical notes I wish more people shared, in plain language, from someone who is in the day to day grind.
